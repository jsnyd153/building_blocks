Applicants must be about to enter their second or penultimate year at any UK or Irish University in order to be eligible to apply for the True North: Discover Bain Workshop.

If you are about to enter your final year of study, you would be welcome to apply for the full-time Associate Consultant role in the London office when applications open in September. For more information, please visit our London Graduate Recruiting page.
